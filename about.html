About Event

AFRO CARIBBEAN VYBE IS AN upscale event will feature a star-studded entertainment line-up, a variety of Caribbean and West-African cuisine, and an enchanted event layout showcasing décor that links Ghana to the Caribbean diaspora.

Afro Caribbean Vybe (ACV) was founded by renowned Caribbean-American event organizers Calvin Chan and Jefferson Selasie Kwame Anyigbanu a Ghanaian entrepreneur. Inspired by their participation in the Year of Return/Beyond the Return events that showcased Ghana's stunning scenery and rich culture, a vision emerged to strengthen the bond between African diaspora communities. ACV's mission is to create a deeper connection between Africa and the Caribbean. December marks the festive season for the Afro-Caribbean diaspora, celebrating and preserving the cultural synergy that leaves a lasting and historic impact.
